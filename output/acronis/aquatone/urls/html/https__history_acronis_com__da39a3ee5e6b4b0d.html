Swiss-Singaporean Company

Acronis is a global leader in cyber protection and hybrid cloud storage. Founded in Singapore in 2003, the company now operates from local offices in 18 different countries, with International Headquarters in Singapore and Corporate Headquarters in Switzerland.

Acronis sets the standard for total cyber protection with its backup, anti-ransomware, disaster recovery, and secure file sync and share solutions. Powered by the Acronis AnyData Engine, artificial intelligence-based ransomware defense, blockchain-based data certification, and more than 100 patents, it delivers easy, complete and affordable cyber protection across any environment—virtual, physical, cloud and mobile. More than 5 million consumers and 500,000 businesses in over 145 countries trust Acronis to protect their data.
